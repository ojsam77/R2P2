AfroBounce

Fast-paced series of movements, targeting different muscle groups that will continuously make you "Bounce" We will be increasing our fitness, focus, and fun.

Dance 101

Will mold how you get in sync with music so that it is a catalyst for repetitive motion. We will establish how you already have rhythm, improve how you listen to music, and expose you to what moves your body is capable of. This will help beginners start moving and stay in motion regardless of what the music is.

Intermediate Dance

Tailored to you as an individual, where we will identify what can make you a better dancer/performer. This is a class for you to polish, your moves, expression, and perception as a dancer. Expect to create 4 custom cerographs by the end of the class.

Advanced Dance

A time to dance like no one is watching. We will push each other to try something new. This is a freestyle class that will boost your confidence, reintroduce your personality, and experience true freedom as a dancer. If you are here you should feel music and use this class as an outlet to keep dance as apart of your life.
